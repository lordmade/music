Data Privacy Policy

At Vynix AI, we prioritize your privacy. Your data is securely stored and used only to enhance your experience. We collect:

  • User profile information (name, email, profile picture) for personalization.
  • Chat history to improve AI responses and maintain conversation context.
  • Uploaded images for analysis, stored securely via Cloudinary.

We do not share your data with third parties except as required for service provision (e.g., Cloudinary for image storage). You can manage your data via the Profile and Settings tabs.
