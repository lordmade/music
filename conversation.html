End-to-End Encryption

Your messages in Vynix AI are protected with end-to-end encryption, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

How it works:

  • Each conversation uses unique keys generated on your device to encrypt messages before sending.
  • Only you and the recipient can decrypt and read the messages—no one else, not even Vynix AI, can access them.
  • Encryption applies to text, images, videos, and calls, keeping your communications secure from interception.
  • Keys change with every message for added protection, and you can verify encryption in chat settings.

This means your private conversations stay private, protected from hackers, governments, or service providers.
